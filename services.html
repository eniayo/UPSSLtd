PHYSICAL PLANNING AND DESIGN

With a growing world population, there comes increased urbanization. In return, the design and use of land is becoming more and more complex and intricate. We are specialists in technical and political aspects of physical planning. Our client-focused solutions ensure that spatial plans are fully incorporated into the fabric of our our communities and market value is maximized through research, analysis and sound engineering principles.
Our key services include:
Master planning:
Integrated township development;
Strategic planning and policy advocacy
Transport planning;
local development framework;
Landscape design;
Heritage and conservation;

Master planning
Our master planning emphasis is on design excellence, policy formulation supported by a wide-range of multidisciplinary skills and technical expertise at every stage of the process. UPSSL multi-disciplinary capability gives us the leverage to address the physical, social and economic aspects of development in a fully integrated approach. We have the reputation of being able to provide imaginative, practical and independent advisory services taking on board project of varying sizes and magnitude for public and private sector clients from inception to completion.

Our holistic approach to understanding the brief, solving the problems and responding to opportunities is pragmatic and innovative to ensure that our solutions are balanced, people friendly and sustainable. Depending on the magnitude of the master plans, each will diagnose, analyse the existing situation with a view to formulating sustainable solutions.

We acknowledge the role of stakeholder and community engagement as central to any project, to gain consensus or preferably active support for proposals. We help the stakeholders involved in the development process to reach consensus on contentious proposals and in delivering practical solutions which can be implemented.

Integrated Development Planning (IDP)

We undertake Integrated Development planning as an approach to involve the entire municipality and its citizens in finding solutions to achieve sustainable long term development. This gives an overall framework for development. Our team of diverse experts are well positioned to provide the solutions to the complex challenges in the urban environment.

Strategic planning and policy advocacy
The emerging rapid urbanization requires innovative urban policies and appropriate advocacy for evolving best practice in physical planning. Our team is position to research into the prevailing government’s institutional and legal framework with a view to provide advice on sustainable urban development policies.

ENVIRONMENTAL ANALYSIS AND AUDIT

We provide a comprehensive range of services to help public and private sector organisations assess, quantify and manage their relationship with the natural environment. Our organization focuses on improving environmental management performance, risk and liability management and competitiveness for our clients. We help business respond to demand for compliance with the environment laws and Regulations.

Environmental Impact Assessment
We deliver on all stages of the EIA process, whether it is the entire EIA or assisting at one or more of the key stages. We support the EIA with specialists from all the relevant environment fields such as noise, air quality, water, ecology, landscape and heritage as well as with technical experts experienced in all of the major infrastructure and developments sectors, such as urban development, energy, waste, water and transport.

FEASIBILITY STUDIES AND PLANNING PERMIT APPROVALS

If you are considering a property or a plot of land or understanding its value from the outset, we are position to provide advisory services for wise investment decision. At all times, we will give you an impartial and honest judgement- advising you on the best strategy and letting you know whether the project is worth pursuing. We recommend our clients start their project with an in-depth desktop feasibility study.
Your Feasibility Study would include a review of:
The site’s context and the local area;
The site’s planning history;
The relevant local and State planning policy
Any major material consideration
Any site specific constraints
Relevant precedent and case law;
Potential opportunities and likelihood of success Strategy for the next stage; Our team include expert cost analysts, economists and estate surveyors and valuers. We also carry out studies and research into housing programme of the government and the housing needs of the society.

Likewise, we offer a broad range of town planning services in the areas of obtaining planning permit approval for our valued clients. We use our professional experience and knowledge with site visits and review of planning framework to provide pre-application framework to provide advice to our clients ensure speedy planning permit outcome. We prepare comprehensive reports in support of planning permit applications, follow up submissions using our healthy professional relationships with the Planning Authorities to ensure smooth statutory process.

Assembling and submitting the application is part of our job, and once submitted we will keep in regular contact with the planning authority official to address queries and resolve an issues as quickly as possible. We co-ordinate the planning application on our client’s behalf, liaising with the architects, preparing a strong case and negotiating with the planning authority officials.


Our team also offer assistance to our clients where rejection of planning applications are considered unreasonable. In such instances, we prepare comprehensive objections on their behalf. We monitor objections throughout the planning authority assessment process and attend consultation meetings on our client’s behalf when appropriate. Our professional expertise ensures that our clients are always afforded the appropriate level of consideration in the decision making process and receive the best possible outcome.


Our team has extensive experience at the Appeals Committee attending hearings on a regular basis on behalf of applicants-clients for review and respondent both in favour of and in objection to proposed developments. We prepare thorough submissions and statements of expert evidence to achieve a high success at the Appeals.


We also help our clients in pre-application advisory services such as obtaining planning information and pre-screening of planning applications for approval. This requires Planning Statement to identify the context and the need for the proposed development along with the argument outlining why the proposal comply with planning policy in basic terms. This assist planning officer in their decision by providing argument for why the scheme is acceptable.

GEOGRAPHIC INFORMATION SYSTEM & MAPPING

Geographic Information System (GIS) is a system designed to capture, store, manipulate, analyze, manage and present all types of geographical data. We have a team of GIS experts who use the system to analyze the trend in urban growth and its direction during expansion with a view to projecting the future. With the appropriate application, GIS can be useful in remote sensing and land surveying. Similarly, we use the system to determine the characteristics and vastness of the land and soil structure within an area.
